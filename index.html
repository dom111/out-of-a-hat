Out of a hat

Anonymously give out items to your friends, no data is stored on the server, just shareable links for your friends.

Best used on mobile, add the items you want your shareable links for (and an optional bit of intro text) and click on the generated links to open a native share window to send via your preferred method. Each link is ticked once the share has been successful so you (Hopefully!) won't send any links twice!
